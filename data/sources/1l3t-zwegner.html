1L3T

On this page there are algs for all 179 cases of 1L3T (1-look last 3 triples) on the FTO. Algs are sorted by OLP (basically, where all the top face's stickers are), then triangle permutation, then corner permutation.

Most of the work in generating these algs and curating them was done by Aedan Bryant (original sheet here). Additionally, most of the TCP algs (set 6c) are from the document maintained by Edd Dibley here. Aside from some minor copyediting and choosing favorites from Aedan's/Edd's sheets, I also selected algs for sets 4a (odd)/4b/6a/6b/a couple from 6c (from algs generated by Aedan).

Notation

These algs start in CIF (corner-in front) notation with yellow on top and blue in front. Possible pre-AUFs are in brackets at the start of algs (like [U']).

S denotes a sledge (R' L R L'), H denotes a hedge (R B' R' B), S' denotes an inverse sledge (L R' L' R), and H' denotes an inverse hedge (B' R B R').

New-style rotation notation: {F1,F2} means put the current F1 face on top, and current F2 face in front (possibly switching to EIF, edge-in-front, in the process). For example {U,BR} is like a y rotation on 3x3, and {U,L} is like half of a y' rotation that switches to EIF.
